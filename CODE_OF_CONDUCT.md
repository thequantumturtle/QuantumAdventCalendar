# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all participants in the Quantum Advent Calendar project to uphold these values.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow in quantum computing

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, nationality, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam or advertisements
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Community events and conferences
- Public representations of the project

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

## Consequences

Depending on the severity:
1. **Warning**: First-time minor violations
2. **Temporary Ban**: Repeated violations (24 hours to 7 days)
3. **Permanent Ban**: Severe or persistent violations

## Questions?

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainers.
